BrowserCMS

BrowserCMS provides Content Management functionality for Rails applications. Once you have installed BrowserCMS into a Rails app, point your browser to localhost:3000/cms to log in. You will be taken to the dashboard, the first section of the BrowserCMS Administration Interface. I will refer to the BrowserCMS Administration Interface as the "CMS", because I am into the whole brevity thing. The four main sections of the CMS are:

Dashboard

The Dashboard shows you a list of tasks that are assigned to you, as well as the quick links.

Site Map

The site map shows you the organization of all sections and pages in your site.

Content Library

The content library is where you view/manage all of the content blocks in your CMS.

Administration

All other tasks, especially ones left to Administrators and Developers, are in this section.
